Details

Gender Distribution

Disproportionate amount of women were accused in comparison to their male counterparts

Marriage Status of Witches

Most of the women accused were married and few accused were actually widowed or single. This may be because married women would have been better off and more wealthy than single women due to their husband or household.

Socioeconomic Status of Witches

Most accused were not part of the extremes of the classes, but were middle class. This was interesting because we were expecting either lower class or upper class citizens to accuse more people.
